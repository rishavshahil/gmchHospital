Service Offers by Hospital

OPD

OPD: Offers routine medical check-ups, consultations, and basic treatments for patients who do not require hospitalization.

IPD

IPD: Provides comprehensive medical care, treatment, and accommodation for patients requiring hospital admission for more than 24 hours.

ICU

ICU: Delivers critical medical care, continuous vital sign monitoring, and essential life support for patients with severe, life-threatening conditions.

Emergency

Emergency: Offers 24/7 immediate medical attention, first aid, and urgent treatment for accidents, injuries, and sudden, serious illnesses.

Blood Bank

Blood Bank: Ensures the constant availability of safe and screened blood and various blood products for necessary transfusions during medical procedures.

Pharmacy

Pharmacy: Dispenses prescribed medications to patients, providing crucial guidance on proper dosage, usage, and potential drug interactions.

Laboratories

Laboratories: Conducts a wide range of essential diagnostic tests on patient samples like blood and urine to aid in accurate disease diagnosis and treatment monitoring.

Ayushman Bharat

Ayushman Bharat It provides cashless access to health insurance services for the beneficiary at the point of service, that is, the hospital.
